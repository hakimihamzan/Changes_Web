BACK TO ENTRIES

It has been a long time coming. I've shed my blood, sweat and (tears). Now it is time to show it. I've been dabling with programming (Android DEV) for quite some time. Now I am ready to create my own stuff and the project that I chose is a workout app. The app that I like to use is called 7 Minute Workout (non-customizable) so I thought to myself,

"Why don't I create one app that people can use to create their own sets of workout and share them with others?"

From Kimi's head

This is what I got. The app is called CHANGES. Users can create their own sets of workout and they'll be able to share them with others. I have my own biases when it comes to workout. For example, I like squats more than I like push-ups. Some people like pull-up more than plank. We all differs. I guess people are opiniated when it comes to certain things.

Why don't I create a place where communities can share their Wout (workout) sets of exercise (whatever they may be). They can rate them or (whatever). And I think something is more important than that is Accountability. If I can provide a place where someone can share his/her goals and other people help keep them accountable to it. That is good enough for me.

At the time of this writing, I've spent 3.5 weeks fiddling with app designs and landing page. I learned a bunch of other tools that could help me better execute this idea. Some of them are Figma, Gimp and Web Dev tools. I will take you through the journey of this app dev, so stay tuned.
